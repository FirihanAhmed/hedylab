# HEDY LAB

by Firihan Ahmed

Where AI meets real-world problem solving.

An independent studio for building AI-powered prototypes, solutions, and frameworks. The work here spans multiple domains — wherever data can be transformed into insight and insight into action.

This is not a research lab. This is where ideas become working systems. Every prototype here is built to demonstrate what is possible when deep domain knowledge meets modern AI. From concept to deployment, from question to answer, from complexity to clarity.

The problems worth solving don't sit inside one field. They live at the intersection of technology, business, regulation, and human experience. That is where this lab operates.

Named after Hedy Lamarr — a self-taught inventor whose patented work became the foundation of Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, and GPS. Her contributions were decades ahead of their time.
